**Architectural Drawing Analysis: A-111_new, Page 1 (Top-Left Quadrant)**

**Region Overview**
This region depicts the northwest corner of the site, showing the intersection of **De Garmo Ave** (North) and **Pendleton Street** (West). It includes the "Front Yard" setback, the corner of the "MRF Building," and a portion of the "(E) Recycling Building (Modified)" containing the "Bale Storage" area.
